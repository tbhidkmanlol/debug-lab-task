Educational Paths

Our Core Programs

Ages 8-14

CS Fundamentals Skills

Discover how to program a computer through coding by creating games and interactive stories and become an expert in a programming language.

Ages 10-16

Coding

Our course combines JavaScript from Beginner to Advanced Level that covered the industrial standards. Learn practical skills for tomorrow's tech world.

Ages 6-12

Web Design

Learn to craft stunning, user-friendly websites from scratch. From layout to coding, we'll equip you with the skills to bring your digital visions to life.

Ages 6-12

Game Development

Dive into coding, design, and storytelling as you craft your own digital adventures. Join us and turn your passion for gaming into a rewarding skillset.

Ages 6-12

3D Printing & Modelling

A great way to introduce children to the creation of prototypes in technology and engineering and improve their problem-solving skills.

Ages 6-12

AR & VR Technology

Embark on a journey into alternate realities with our AR & VR course. From gaming to education, discover the boundless applications of immersive technology.
